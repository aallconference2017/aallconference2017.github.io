Registration will officially open on December 1st, 2016. Please find information below on all registration options available, all registrations are inclusive of 10% GST. 

Member Registrations

Early Bird $440 (closes September 15th, 2017) 
Standard $495

Non Member Registrations

Early Bird $470 (closes September 15th, 2017)
Standard $540

Virtual Registrations

Virtual Registrations $250 
This will offer a single stream of presentations only and be limited to 100 participants. Virtual sessions will be scheduled across all days of the conference. 

Group Registrations

For organisations who register 5 or more staff at the one time a 10% registration discount will be made available at the time of payment. 

Member and Non Member Registration Includes

  • Full conference program
  • All social functions

Payments

All payments must be made in Australian dollars and received prior to the conference. If payment is not received prior to the conference you will be asked to provide your personal credit card details to confirm your registration. A tax (GST) invoice will be emailed to you once your registration is confirmed. 

Cancellation of Registration

Notification of cancellation must be made in writing to the Conference Secretariat. A $110 cancellation fee (GST Inc.) will apply for conference registrations cancelled up until September 30, 2017. After this date no refunds will be issued.
